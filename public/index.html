Swimbot Speak (v0.3)

Here you can have a conversation with a swimbot. Ask them whatever you'd like and follow up on your questions. They have some memory, so they will be able to keep track of the conversation (up to a certain extent!). Also, be sure to wait a few seconds after clicking submit. Swimbots need a bit of time to think!

Be creative! For example, you can ask questions as if you are a swimbot interacting with them as well. Try to build a friendship. See what secrets or special places they will show or tell you about. Can you make a new friend? You might be surprised about what they have to share with you.

You can also play around with what the swimbot is thinking about most right now. See the sliders below and some instructions on how to use them.
